Welcome to Decycle

Where Today's Waste Becomes Tomorrow's Fuel

Turning Waste into Fuel

At Decycle, we harness the power of the economy's waste to fuel its future. By transforming discarded tyres, plastic waste, and used motor oil into valuable fuel, we aim to clean up our communities and provide affordable energy solutions by creating a circular economy that benefits both the environment and society.

About Us

Decycle is committed to addressing the pressing issue of waste management while providing sustainable and cost-effective fuel alternatives. We focus on:

Environmental Cleanup: Our innovative processes remove harmful waste from poor communities, improving living conditions and reducing pollution.

Sustainable Fuel Production: By converting waste into fuel, we offer a greener alternative that helps reduce reliance on traditional fossil fuels.

Economic Empowerment: Our solutions not only clean the environment but also create job opportunities and support local economies.

Join us in our mission to create a cleaner, more sustainable world. Together, we can turn today's waste into tomorrow's energy.

Decycle is the brainchild of Nanda Dulal Das Athmanandh, a dedicated young entrepreneur committed to addressing societal challenges in South Africa. Our primary goal at Decycle is to establish a circular economy that not only mitigates environmental impact but also creates value from waste materials, specifically plastic and tyres. Decycle operates on the principle of sustainability and profitability. By extracting maximum value from waste materials, we ensure that every step of our production process minimizes environmental footprint. Our commitment to efficiency and eco-friendly practices drives our mission to provide affordable fuel alternatives while actively preserving our planet's natural resources.
